Research

Research Interests

Partial Differential Equations and Geometric Analysis, including nonlinear elliptic PDEs, geometric measure theory.

Recently, I was interested in following topics:

  • Qualitative properties of semilinear elliptic partial differential equations
  • Application of geometric measure theory in elliptic PDEs, such as calibration and blowing-down
  • Nonlinear instability of Navier-Stokes equations and Bootstrap method
