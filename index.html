Research

Research Interests

Partial Differential Equations and Geometric Analysis, including nonlinear elliptic PDEs, geometric measure theory.

Recently, I was interested in following topics:

  • [November 2025 - now] Double-exponential growth of Euler equations in 2D, including the basic framework introduced by Kiselev-Sverak's work in 2014, called the small scale creation, however, recently, Andrej Zlatos, 6 July 2025, showed the desired growth on the half plane by a new idea, which extended the original method to unbounded domains.
  • [October 2025 - now] Nonlinear instability of Navier-Stokes equations, including the linear stability established in Yudovich's book, the Bootstrap method established by Strauss-Guo in 1990s and its application in Friedlander-Pavlović-Shvydkoy's paper in 2006.
  • [October 2025 - now] Convergence of minimal surfaces, including weak convergence and existence problem (see Colding-Minicozzi's book chapter 3-4), min-max method and its applications (see Colding-Minicozzi's book chapter 5 and Colding-De Lellis's survey in 2003). I recommend the beginners to study Fischer-Schoen's work in 1985 and Fischer-Schoen's work in 1990. On the other hand, B. White's note and Chodosh's note are also useful.
  • [September 2025 - October 2025] Qualitative properties of semilinear elliptic partial differential equations, including the symmetry of semilinear equations, Liouville-type theorem of certain Schrödinger operator and energy estimate, motivated by Berestycki-Caffarelli-Nirenberg's work in 1997 and Alberti-Ambrosio-Cabré's work in 2001.
  • The regularity of elliptic partial differential equations, including Schauder estimate in Wang's paper in 2006, which can be extended to more general cases, such as the parabolic equations and fully nonlinear elliptic equations; However, I want to show the application on semilinear equations with certain convexity, which is inspired by Caffarelli-Friedman's work in 1985 and Caffarelli-Guan-Ma's work in 2006.
